Old Bay Cod

Old Bay Cod

Dietary
Oil Free
Sugar Free
Low Carb
Calories per serving
150
Servings
1
Ingredients

5 oz cod
1/2 T Homemade Old Bay Seasoning (or store bought Old Bay)

Directions
  1. Line baking sheet with parchment paper and preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Place cod on baking sheet and cover with Homemade Old Bay Seasoning
  3. Bake 12-15 minutes, until fish flakes easily with a fork
