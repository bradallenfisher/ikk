Zingy Swordfish Salad

Zingy Swordfish Salad

Dietary
Oil Free
Sugar Free
Low Carb
Calories per serving
215
Servings
1
Ingredients

1 serving Zingy Asian Dressing
8 oz romaine lettuce
3.5 oz broiled swordfish, chilled

Directions
  1. Cut chilled swordfish into bite sized pieces 
  2. Place lettuce and swordfish in salad bowl
  3. Toss with Zingy Asian Dressing
